# Nell Gram | Documentation Portfolio

## About me

Nell is a Senior Technical Writer contracted through Accenture to Google, where she writes documentation for Google Play's 2+ billion users and manages a team of 5 globally distributed writers.

Her work spans public-facing help documentation, internal knowledge base articles, and agent-focused content such as announcements and templated responses.

### Key Responsibilities

**Project and People Management**
Nell oversees documentation from initial design through quality assurance post-publication, collaborating with stakeholders and directing her distributed team through project cycles.

**Cross-Functional Collaboration**
She partners with content strategists, product managers, legal departments, and usability specialists to refine both public and internal documentation.

**Content Systems Design**
Her background in Information Science informs her approach to structure and metadata. She develops team style guides, creates project requirement templates, and ensures content remains discoverable and useful long-term.

**Usability and Accessibility Focus**
She actively tests procedures and documentation, reviews standard operating procedures for clarity, and has professional experience making content accessible for people with disabilities.

**AI Training and Editing**
Nell trains AI systems through prompt engineering to generate documentation, then edits outputs to align with team standards and style guidelines.

### Background

Originally from Los Angeles, Nell studied English in Ohio before pursuing graduate studies in corporate research. Her career evolved toward professional writing, where she continues applying her research background to information organization and architecture.

**Current Status:** Remote, based in Los Angeles; planning East Coast relocation
