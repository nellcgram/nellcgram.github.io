# About me

Hi, I'm Nell. I'm a Senior Technical Writer for Accenture on contract with Google. I write documentation for Google Play's 2+ billion users and manage a team of 5 globally distributed writers.

Specifically, I write and edit public-facing help documentation, internal knowledge base guides, and agent-facing content like announcements and canned responses. This site includes sample documentation I've written.

## The details

My job also has several aspects beyond writing:

**I manage projects and people.** As a writer, I manage projects from initial documentation design through post-publish Quality Assurance. I meet with stakeholders through the process to clarify project requirements and address issues. As a manager, I assign projects to my team and guide them through project life cycles.

**I edit in collaboration with cross-functional teams.** I work with content strategists, product managers, legal teams, and usability experts to edit both public-facing and agent-facing documentation. I love balancing each team's needs with finding the right language to describe what an application actually does.

**I design content.** I build the systems that make content possible. This might mean creating a style guide for the team or writing a template for project requirements. My Information Science degree reflects my love of making information searchable; I am your go-to person to fix an article's structure with the right information architecture or add metadata before publication. I consider how content will hold up over time and how to design it so it stays useful.

**I'm passionate about usability and accessibility.** I test documentation and procedures by actually using them. Some days I review SOPs to find what's confusing. Others, I collaborate with engineers to improve a new project management software before rollout. I have previous professional experience in making content accessible for users with disabilities, which is often useful in my current work.

**I'm an AI trainer and editor.** I train AI systems to generate documentation on a topic through prompt engineering, then edit the output to align with the team's style guide and standards.

I hope this description gives you a fuller picture of my work than a resume can capture, though you'll find the obligatory resume below.
