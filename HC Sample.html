Chronicle App Store personalization and data

In the app, you can use the “Your Data in Chronicle” screen to control your personalization settings. This article explains personalization preferences, the benefits of personalization, and consent management.

Computer content selector | Phone content selector

Edit your personalization settings

Personalization can improve search results and recommendations in Chronicle based on information in your account. You can control which kinds of data are used to personalize results.

  1. Open the Chronicle Store app.
  2. Tap your Profile icon > Your data in Chronicle.
  3. Go to personalization settings at the top of the page.
    • Find “Personalization” and toggle the switch to off > Turn off
    • Or go to “more personalization options" to change device, payment information, or entitlements

Turn off all personalization in Chronicle

If you turn off this setting, Chronicle won’t customize your experience to better fit your interests.

What will change:

  • Search results may seem more general
  • App, game, and book recommendations may be less relevant to your interests
  • Offers may also be less relevant to your interests

To do this in the app:

  1. Open the Chronicle store.
  2. Tap your Profile icon > Your data in Chronicle
  3. Find “Personalization” and toggle the switch to off > Turn off
Tip: If you want to manage data collection in your account, you can keep the Personalization toggle on but control which kinds of data are used for personalization.

Control your personalized experience in Chronicle

If you don’t want to turn personalization on for all data types, you can control which types of data in your account Chronicle can use to personalize results and recommendations for you instead.

  1. Open the Chronicle Store.
  2. Tap your Profile icon > Your data in Chronicle > More personalization options

Device information

  • Chronicle can use information about your device to improve personalization
  • You can turn the device information toggle on or off

Payment methods

  • Chronicle can use information about your payment methods to personalize recommendations and offers.
  • You can turn the payment methods toggle on or off.

Your Chronicle content

  • Chronicle can personalize your experience based on items you’ve bought or acquired through Chronicle, such as apps, books, or games.
    • Click Manage to personalize this, then switch on or off for each item.
  • You can tell Chronicle not to use specific items for personalization. Your changes may take up to 24 hours to take effect.

Other Personalization Controls

To control how other Chronicle services personalize your experience:

  • Visit The Ad Center to change how Chronicle services use information to personalize ads
  • Visit My Account to change personalization and security settings for your Chronicle Account
